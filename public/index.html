Fresh Zincs

The Fresh Zincs Platform is the first end-to-end blockchain-powered marketplace designed exclusively for pre-owned marine vessels, delivering unparalleled data integrity and transparency. Every listing on Fresh Zincs is backed by a rigorously standardized survey commissioned by the seller and conducted only by certified, network-vetted surveyors. Those digital surveys are cryptographically signed and immutably recorded on a high-throughput blockchain (e.g., Polygon), so buyers can instantly verify authenticity and provenance with zero reliance on third-party intermediaries. Leveraging advanced optical character recognition (OCR), Fresh Zincs automatically ingests every line item from survey reports—hull dimensions, engine hours, structural findings, safety equipment inventories, and more—transforming static PDFs into a fully searchable data lake. This granular search capability lets serious buyers filter vessels by exact criteria (e.g., “engine hours < 500” or “no structural defects noted”), dramatically reducing unqualified inquiries. By enforcing a “surveys-first” sales model, the platform cuts in-person visits by up to 50%, streamlining transactions and elevating buyer confidence. With Fresh Zincs, sellers benefit from faster, higher-quality leads, and buyers gain access to a transparent, tamper-proof marketplace where every detail is verified, indexed, and effortlessly discoverable, redefining how the world buys and sells marine vessels.
