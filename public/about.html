About Fresh Zincs

Fresh Zincs is a blockchain-based marketplace for used marine vessels, specifically for serious inquiries only, utilizing the blockchain to do so.

Meet Sam Rauch

Sam Rauch is a seasoned blockchain architect and technology leader. Through his consultancy, SSR Technologies, he designs scalable blockchain solutions and pioneered the “Proof Framework” for immutable verification across enterprise systems. Previously as Director of Development and Blockchain Solutions at RiseIT Solutions, Sam delivered smart-contract-driven platforms, and today he advises on curriculum as a board member of the Blockchain Institute of Technology. His deep expertise in distributed ledger architectures will ensure Fresh Zincs’ surveys are trusted, transparent, and tamper-proof.
